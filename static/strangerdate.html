How it works

Three steps to your next great conversation

No profiles. No setup. No swiping. Just click Start and you're live with a real stranger in seconds.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

HD Video Chat

Crystal-clear video powered by WebRTC. See every smile, every raised eyebrow, every genuine laugh — in real time.

Global Reach

Connect with strangers from 190+ countries. Practice a language, discover a culture, or just see the world from your room.

Interest Tags

Add optional tags like music, travel, or philosophy. We'll find someone who shares them — or challenges them.
